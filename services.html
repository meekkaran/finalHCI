Case Reporting

A victim reports a case in a written document stating what happenned in detail. Each case report is a legal document that becomes a permanent written record of that event or incident.

Investigative report

An investigative report is a written document prepared by a peace officer that records in detail the officer's observations and actions as they relate to a specific event or incident. Each investigative report is a legal document that becomes a permanent written record of that event or incident.
